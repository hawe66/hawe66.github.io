Research

I'm broadly interested in cognition, and especially in decision-making scenarios where understanding the environment and applying the prior knowledge is needed. I believe this framework is scalable to interpret many things in real world. Based on this interest, I am studying and working on reinforcement learning to design the model which learns the causal structure of the environment and applies prior knowledge based on its understanding. Towards this end, I want to examine 'how' intelligence acquires these abilities and uses them, and explain the intelligence within this framework.
